Robotics

## About

*   Secretariat: [SIS](https://www.iso.org/member/2101.html "Swedish Institute for Standards (Sweden)")(Sweden) Committee Manager: [Ms Katarina Widström](mailto:katarina.widstrom@sis.se)

## Scope

Standardization in the field of robotics, excluding toys and military applications
