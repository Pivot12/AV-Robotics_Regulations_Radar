# Autonomous Vehicles

## I'm looking for…

![Image 4: Car](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/uploads/2022/12/car.svg)

### Autonomous Vehicle Regulations

Since autonomous vehicle (AV) technology first began raising regulatory considerations in 2013, the California DMV has worked to develop testing and deployment regulations.

[Explore AV Regulations](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/vehicle-industry-services/autonomous-vehicles/california-autonomous-vehicle-regulations/)

### Reporting AV Incidents

Learn how to report autonomous vehicle incidents and fulfill required reporting obligations. Find reporting requirements, submission instructions, and resources for permit holders to help ensure compliance with California DMV regulations.

[Learn how to report an incident](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/vehicle-industry-services/autonomous-vehicles/autonomous-vehicles-incident-reporting/)

### Testing Permit Holders

The California DMV authorizes manufacturers to test and deploy autonomous vehicles under three permit categories: testing with a safety driver, driverless testing, and deployment. Each category has specific approved locations, operating conditions, and speed limits.

[See current permit holders](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/vehicle-industry-services/autonomous-vehicles/autonomous-vehicle-testing-permit-holders/)

### Permit Resources

The program provides resources for autonomous vehicle (AV) permit holders, including regulations, application guides, and required reporting templates.

[View permit resources](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/vehicle-industry-services/autonomous-vehicles/autonomous-vehicles-program-permit-resources/)

### Collision Reports

Manufacturers testing autonomous vehicles need to report every collision involving one of their vehicles that results in property damage, bodily injury, or death.

[View collision reports](https://tims.berkeley.edu/tools/avsafety.php)

### Law Enforcement Resources

The program provides resources for Law Enforcement, including training materials and form ordering instructions.

[See law enforcement resources](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/vehicle-industry-services/autonomous-vehicles/law-enforcement-resources/)
