Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

AV & Robotics Standards Radar is built to be useful and respectful. We collect as little as possible, we never sell your data, and we tell you plainly what we do.

Anonymous usage logging

When you visit, we log anonymous, aggregate usage signals to understand whether the tool is useful and working:

  • Timezone (e.g. America/Los_Angeles)
  • Browser language (e.g. en-US)
  • Referrer — the page that linked you here, if any
  • Page path visited and a rough time-on-page duration

This data is not tied to your identity and is used only to improve the product.

Email digest & contact data

If you sign up for the digest or use the contact form, we collect your name and email address (and your message, for the contact form).

  • Digest details are used only to send you regulation- and standards-change updates.
  • Contact details are used only to respond to your message.
  • We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
  • Ask us any time to remove your data and we will.

Built on public regulatory & standards sources

AV & Robotics Standards Radar monitors publicly available official sources — government regulators and standards bodies governing autonomous vehicles, robotics, functional safety, AI governance, and drones. We detect that a source page changed and link you to the official source. We do not host or republish the regulations or standards themselves, and detected changes are an indicator to investigate — always confirm the substance with the official authority.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Use the contact form — it reaches us directly, no inbox-hunting required.
